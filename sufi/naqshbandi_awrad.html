Naqshbandi Awrad/zikr to be recited every 24 hours.
PART ONE - COMPULSORY
  1. 3 times: SHAHADA

    Ash-hadu al la ilaha ill-Allah wadahu la shareeka lahu, wa ash-hadu anna Muhammadan abduhu wa Rasuluhu, salla-Allah ta'ala alayhi wa-sallim.

  2. 70 times: Astaghfirullah
  3. Once: SURAT AL-FATIHA

    A'auzu Billahi min-ash-shaitan-nir-rajim, Bismilla-Hir-Rahman-Nir-Rahim. Al-hamdu lil lahi Rabbil a'ala-min, Ar-Rahman Nir-Rahim, Maliki yaw-mid-din, Iyyaka nabudu wa'iyyaka nastaeen, Ih'di-nas siraatal mustaqeem, Sir'atal-ladhina an-amta alai-him, Ghai-ril magh'dhubi alai-him wa-ladh dha'lin. (Ameen)

  4. Once: AYAT AAMANA'R-RASUL

    Bismilla-Hir-Rahman-Nir-Rahim. Aa-mana'r-Rasuulu bimaa un-zila ilay-hi-mir-Rabbihii wal mu' minuun, kullun aa-mana Billaahi wa malaa-ikatihii wa kutubi-hii wa Rasullih, laa nufarriqu bay-na ahdim-mir-Rasullih, wa qaa-luu sami-na wa ata-na gufraanaka rabbana wa ilaykal-masiir, laa yukalli-fullaahu naf-san illaa wus-'ahaa, lahaa maa kasabat wa'alay-haa mak-tasabat, Rabbana laa tu-aa-khizna inna-sinaa aw akha-naa, Rabbana wa laa tah-mil alaynaa is-ran-kam'aa hamal-ta-huu, alal-laziina min-qablina, Rabbana wa la tuhamilnaa maa laa taaqata lanaa bih, wa-fu'annaa, wag-fir-lanaa, war-ham-naa'anta mawlanaa fan-surnaa 'alal-qaw-mil kaafi-riin.

  5. 7 times: SURAT AL-INSHIRAH

    Bismilla-Hir-Rahman-Nir-Rahim. Alam nashrah laka sadrak, wawad'ana anka wizrak, al-ladhee anka-dha dh'rak, warafa'ana laka dhik'rak, fa'inna ma'al usri yusra, inna ma'al usri yusra, fa'idha faragta fansab, wa ilaa-Rabbika farghab.

  6. 11 times: SURAT AL-IKHLAS

    Bismilla-Hir-Rahman-Nir-Rahim. Qul huwa Allahu Ahad, Allahu Samad, lam yalid walam yulad, walam yakum lahu qufu-wan Ahad.

  7. Once: SURAT AL-FALAQ

    Bismilla-Hir-Rahman-Nir-Rahim. Qul a'auzu bi'Rabbil falaq, min-shar'ri ma'a khalaq, wa min shar'ri gasiqin idha-waqab, wa min shar'rin-naffa' sati fil-uqad, wa min shar'ri ha'sidin idha-hasad.

  8. Once: SURAT AL-AN'NAS

    Bismilla-Hir-Rahman-Nir-Rahim. Qul a'auz bi'Rabbin-nas, ma'lilin-nas, ilahin-nas, min shar'ril was'wasil kha-nas, al'ladhee yuwas-wisu fee sudurin-nas, minal jin'nati wan-nas.

  9. 9 times: La ilaha ill-Allah.
  10. Once: La ilaha ill-Allah Muhammadun Rasul-Allah.
  11. 10 times: Allahumma salli ala Muhammadin wa ala alee Muhammadin wa sallim.
  12. Once: Present this Awrad/Zikr to The Holy Prophet Muhammed (S.A.W.) and to all the Prophets and Saint, particularly to Shah Naqshband, Sultan-ul-Awliya Sheikh Abdullah ad-Daghestani and our Master Mawlana Sheikh Muhammed Nazim al-Haqqani ar-Rabbani by reciting the Idha followed by Surat al-Fatiha.
    1. IHDA: Ila shara'fin Nabiyyi sallal Allahu alayhi wa sallim, wa alihi wa sahbihi, wa ila arwahi ikhwanihi mina'l-anbiya'i wal-mursalina, wa khudaam'ee shara'ihim wa ila arwahil a'immat'il arba'a, wa ila arwahi mashyikhina fi't-tariqatin Naqshbandiyaa'til Aliyyah, khassatan ila ruhi-immami't-tariqa wa gauthil-khaliqa Khwaja Shah Bahauddin Naqshband Muhammad-il-Uwasiyy-il-Bukhari, wa ila Sultan-ul-Awliya Sheihk Abdullah al-Faiz ad-Daghestani wa ila Mawlana Sultan-ul-Awliya Sheikh Muhammad Nazim al-Haqqani ar-Rabbani.
    2. Once: SURAT AL-FATIHA

      Bismilla-Hir-Rahman-Nir-Rahim. Al-hamdu lil lahi Rabbil a'ala-min, Ar-Rahman Nir-Rahim, Maliki yaw-mid-din, Iyyaka nabudu wa'iyyaka nastaeen, Ih'di-nas siraatal mustaqeem, Sir'atal-ladhina an-amta alai-him, Ghai-ril magh'dhubi alai-him wa-ladh dha'lin. (Ameen)

  13. 3 times: Allahu-Allahu-Allahu-Haq.
  14. 1500/5000 times: Allah Allah Allah....
  15. 300/2000 times: Allahumma salli ala Muhammadin wa ala alee Muhammadin wa salim.
  16. 100 times: One Juzu (Portion) from Holy Qur'an, or 100 times Surat Al-Ikhlas.
  17. One Hizb (Part) of Dalil-ul-Khayrat, or 100 times Salawat.
PART TWO - NOT COMPULSORY - OPTIONAL
  1. 100/1000 times: La ilaha illal Allah.
  2. 100/700 times: Astaghfir-ullah-hal-Azeem wa atu'ubu illaihi.
  3. 101 times: Ya Hayyu, ya Qayyum bi-Rahmatika-Astagith.
  4. 100 times: Ya Fatthu, ya Alimu, ya Qawiyyu, ya Matin.
  5. 100 times: La ilaha illal Allahul Maleekul Haq-ul-Mubeen, Muhammad-dur-Rasullahi sadiq-ul-wadul-Ameen.
